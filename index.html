What is ONAtrail?

A research-driven platform for trail runners

ONAtrail is a web platform developed as part of the MAGIC-Trail research project, led by Dr. Nicolas Bouscaren at the Reunion University Hospital (CHU de La Réunion). It combines practical tools for trail runners with the opportunity to contribute to scientific research on trail running and endurance sports.

Race preparation

Plan your races with course-aware tools designed for trail and ultra-trail events.

Pacing optimization

Personalized pacing strategies based on your training history and race profiles.

Performance analysis

Detailed analysis of your activities: pace, elevation, heart rate and training load.

Scientific research

Optionally contribute your data to research on trail running health and performance.

Strava & Garmin Integration

How ONAtrail connects to your training services

Strava

With your explicit authorization, ONAtrail synchronizes your Strava activities (distance, duration, elevation gain, GPS tracks, pace, heart rate) to power race preparation, pacing and performance analysis features.

Authorization uses Strava's official OAuth flow — we never see your Strava password — and you can revoke ONAtrail's access at any time from your Strava settings ("My Apps") or from your ONAtrail account.

Garmin

With your explicit authorization, ONAtrail synchronizes activity and wellness data from Garmin Connect (activities, heart rate, and when available heart-rate variability, recovery and sleep metrics) through the official Garmin Connect Developer Program APIs.

Authorization uses Garmin's official OAuth flow — we never see your Garmin password — and you can revoke ONAtrail's access at any time from your Garmin Connect account settings or from your ONAtrail account.

Your data stays under your control. Synchronized data is used only for the purposes described in our Privacy Policy. Health-related data is processed only with your explicit consent, is hosted by an HDS-certified health data host, and is never sold. You can disconnect a service or request deletion of your data at any time.

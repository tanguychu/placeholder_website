About ONAtrail

ONAtrail is a specialized trail running performance web platform designed to support athletes before, during, and after their races. Through Strava integration, athletes access trail-specific tools:

Trail race preparation

Pacing assistance based on course characteristics and historical race performance data.

Performance analytics

Elevation-based performance metrics and detailed post-race analysis.

Athlete profile

Climbing and descending ability analysis to identify individual strengths and weaknesses.

Strava integration

All Strava data is used solely to provide insights to the authenticated athlete within their own account.

The platform is currently in its final development phase, with a public launch planned for August 14, 2026. You can preview the interface in our interactive Figma prototype (work in progress — not the final design).

Connecting your Strava account

  1. Log in to your ONAtrail account.
  2. Click Connect with Strava in your account settings.
  3. You will be redirected to Strava's official authorization page. Review the requested permissions and click Authorize.
  4. You're done — your activities will be synchronized automatically and your trail analytics will start appearing in your dashboard.

ONAtrail never sees or stores your Strava password. Authorization is handled entirely by Strava through its official OAuth flow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to connect my Strava account?

Connecting your Strava account lets ONAtrail analyze your training history to provide personalized trail analytics: pacing recommendations, climbing and descending profiles, and race preparation tools tailored to you.

What data is imported from Strava?

Only the activity data needed to provide your insights: activities with distance, duration, elevation gain, GPS tracks, pace and heart rate when available. Your data is only ever visible to you, within your own account. We never sell your data and we never share it with third parties for advertising.

How can I disconnect my Strava account?

You can revoke ONAtrail's access at any time, in either of two ways:

  • From ONAtrail: open your account settings and click Disconnect Strava.
  • From Strava: go to Settings → My Apps on strava.com and click Revoke Access next to ONAtrail.

Once access is revoked, synchronization stops immediately.

How can I delete my data?

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from your account settings or by emailing us at ona.trail@outlook.com. See our Privacy Policy for details on data retention.

My activities are not syncing. What should I do?

First, check that ONAtrail still appears in Settings → My Apps on strava.com. If it does and syncing still fails, contact us at ona.trail@outlook.com and we will help you resolve the issue.

ONAtrail

Advanced trail running analytics and race preparation platform

ONAtrail is a web platform dedicated to trail runners, helping athletes prepare for races, analyze their performances, and gain personalized insights from their training activities through Strava integration.

Main Features

🏔️

Trail race preparation

Optimized pacing based on course characteristics and historical race data.

📈

Performance analytics

Post-race analysis and trail-specific performance metrics.

💪

Athlete profile

Identify strengths and weaknesses in climbing, descending, and endurance.

🔗

Strava integration

Securely connect your Strava account to access personalized insights.

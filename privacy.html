Privacy Policy – ONAtrail

Effective Date: 01 November 2025

1. About ONAtrail

ONAtrail is a web platform developed as part of the MAGIC-Trail research project led by Dr. Nicolas Bouscaren at CHU Réunion and funded through the European FEDER program.

The platform provides trail runners with tools for race preparation, performance analysis, pacing optimization, training monitoring, and participation in research initiatives related to trail running and endurance sports.

2. Data We Collect

Depending on the services you choose to use, we may collect:

  • Account information (name, email address, age, sex).
  • Garmin and Strava account identifiers required for synchronization.
  • Activity data such as distance, duration, elevation gain, GPS tracks, pace, heart rate, training load, and other available performance metrics.
  • Health-related data made available through connected services and authorized by the user.
  • Responses to optional research questionnaires.

We do not collect your Garmin or Strava login credentials.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data may be used to:

  • Display and analyze your activities within ONAtrail.
  • Provide personalized trail-running insights and recommendations.
  • Generate race preparation, pacing, and performance analysis tools.
  • Improve existing platform features.
  • Conduct scientific and research activities related to trail running and endurance sports.
  • Ensure platform security and proper operation.

4. Artificial Intelligence Transparency

ONAtrail may use activity and health-related data to develop, train, improve, or operate data analysis, prediction, and recommendation models.

Such use will only occur with the user's explicit consent when required by applicable regulations.

Users may withdraw this consent at any time, without affecting their access to core platform functionalities.

5. Data Sharing and Sale of Data

ONAtrail does not sell individual user data to third parties.

Data may be used in aggregated and anonymized form for research, statistical analysis, and platform improvement purposes.

Where required by law or regulation, users will be informed and appropriate consent mechanisms will be provided.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the services requested by the user, comply with legal obligations, or support approved research activities.

Users may request deletion of their account and associated personal data at any time.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you may:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to certain processing activities.
  • Request export of your data.

Requests may be submitted using the contact information below.

8. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

9. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updated versions will be published on this page.
